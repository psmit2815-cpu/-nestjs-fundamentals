About Td

TuteDude is an online learning platform founded by IIT Delhi alumni on June 15, 2019. It offers courses in web development, data science, app development, and UI/UX design. The platform focuses on providing personalized mentorship, doubt-solving sessions, and practical learning through hands-on projects. TuteDude aims to make learning interactive and skill-oriented for students across India.

One of its main highlights is the 100% refund guarantee, which allows learners to get their full fee back upon completing all assignments and projects within the set time limit. This approach motivates students to finish their courses while ensuring consistent effort and discipline. The platform also provides guided mentorship and project-based assessments for real-world learning.

However, users should carefully read the refund terms, as it only applies if all conditions are met. Some students have praised TD for its support and affordability, while others found issues with communication and clarity. Overall, it's a good option for motivated learners looking for guided, budget-friendly online skill development.

Trivia & Interesting Facts

Did You Know?

Why 100% Refund Guarantee?

TuteDude's unique refund policy was designed to motivate students to complete their courses. Statistics show that students who commit to finishing all assignments have a 95% higher success rate in landing jobs compared to those who don't complete their coursework!

Success Stories

Over 50,000+ students have enrolled in TuteDude courses, with more than 10,000 successfully transitioning to tech careers. Many have been placed in top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and various startups across India.

The IIT Delhi Connection

Founded by IIT Delhi alumni who understood the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, TuteDude was created to bridge this gap with practical, project-based learning. The founders themselves mentor select batches of students!

Learning Statistics

Research shows that project-based learning increases knowledge retention by 75% compared to traditional lecture-based methods. That's why every TuteDude course includes multiple real-world projects that students can add to their portfolios.
